water pollution







Water pollution may occur when waste water is discharged into clean water bodies without undergoing proper treatment to nullify the effects of lethal chemicals. At times people consume this water and it leads to various water- borne diseases such as typhoid and cholera.Water pollution" is a term used when water in places like rivers, lakes, or oceans gets dirty because of either stuff people do or natural happenings. Today, water pollution is a big worry for the environment, and we need to handle it carefully. Water is the most important component of life, which is needed by human beings and needed for animals, birds, trees, etc
